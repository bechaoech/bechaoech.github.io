No matter how it‚Äôs cooked, it just wouldn‚Äôt feel right without that salt-and-chili dip made the Southwestern Vietnam way.

Nui Thit Vien

A simple bowl of warmth for our first cold evening in Maryland.

Nui thi vien is what I often make on rainy days. When it rains, the weather gets colder, and I usually feel a bit lazier than usual. So when I crave something warm, quick, and comforting, nui thit vien is always my go-to choice. Since it‚Äôs made with macaroni, you can easily eat it with just a spoon ‚Äî no chopsticks needed like with pho, mi, or hu tieu.
